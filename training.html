How to run this block

Sessions, not weekdays. Two leg days + push + pull are the fixed four; pump is the optional fifth. Slot them where the week allows, inside these guardrails:

Guardrails. Legs · heavy goes Mon or Tue (concentric bias recovers fast — volleyball survives; Tuesday evening after volleyball also works). Legs · ecc goes Fri or Sat — its DOMS must land on the weekend, never within ~72 h of Tuesday volleyball. Keep 3+ clear days between the two leg days. Push and pull fill Wed/Thu in either order. Pump goes anywhere, morning or evening.

Dropping order on a heavy week: pump first, then push. Never a leg day — and guard the pull day; it's the most goal-relevant upper session.

Supersets are suggestions. Pair only when the stations sit together; otherwise run straight sets.

Progression (double progression). Stay at a load until you hit the top of the rep range on all sets at the target RIR, then add: +2.5–5 kg lower body, +1–2.5 kg upper. Pull-ups: reps first, then weight.

Right side first. Lead every single-leg/arm move with the right; match the left. Right feels weaker? One extra set on the right — never the left.

Knee. The wall sits + tempo work on Legs · ecc are treatment, not threat — tendons like regular, controlled load. Skip the lateral bound any day the knee is a 2+.

Leg-day warm-up. The 10-move stretch flow (30–45 s each, now checkable), tendon prep on Legs · ecc, then ramp-up sets: ~50% and ~75% of work weight on the first lift, one light set before each later lift. Short on time: cat-cow, downward dog, world's greatest stretch, figure four, supine twists.

Deload when the app suggests it (knee flaring, or 5+ weeks hard): keep loads, cut every exercise to 2 sets, skip throws and bounds for the week.

RIR = reps in reserve. RIR 2 ≈ stop 2 shy of failure. Log your top set only.
